Kiwi Puppet

A Kiwi produced for Top Pocket Pop-Up Theatre at Redfest in Bristol.

The cable control mechanism gives the puppet hip and knee joints flexibility while not affecting the controls.

  • PVC Foamboard
  • Closed-Cell Foam
