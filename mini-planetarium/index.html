Planetarium

A planetarium, able to project all eight planets onto a paper lampshade.

The mini-planetarium is controlled by mobile, over wi-fi network, using the Open Sound Control (OSC) protocol.

  • Touch Designer
  • TouchOSC
  • Open Sound Control
  • Projector
Interface in TouchOSC controls the angle, speed of rotation and the projected planet.
