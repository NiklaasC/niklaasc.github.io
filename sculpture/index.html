Sculpture

Izzy Bristow started the 2017 Year of Sculpture as a way to improve her sculpting ability by producing a sculpture every week in 2017.

I have joined in with the fun: Randomly selecting one of 52 prompts, as a way of expanding the range of subjects.

I have also used the challenge as an opportunity to practise with different materials.
