Tinkering Sign

An interactive sign that responds to visitors of at-Bristol, and guides them to the Tinkering Space.

The letters flip as visitors pass by the sign, and resync after a timeout period

  • Laser-cut Acrylic
  • Laser-cut HIPS
  • DC Motors
  • Microcontrollers
