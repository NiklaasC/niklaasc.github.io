Manifold

A robotic rabbit used to explore the psychological phenomenon of Mirroring as an instinctual, initiating interaction between robots and humans.

Manifold uses machine vision to track the head tilt of a viewer and a three degree-of-freedom neck mechanism mirrors the viewers head movement.

  • 3D Modeling
  • Paper
  • Resin
  • PVC Foamboard
  • Microcontroller
  • Servos
  • Machine Vision - Kinect and Touch Designer
Paper is a fantastic rapid prototyping material. Many iterations can be designed, fabricated and assembled in a day.
Paper treated with resin can rapidly produce a robust enclosure.
