Rock Cam

A camera trap disguised as a rock for natural history documentary, filming the release of chimpanzees. It was necessary to secure the camera and make the trap extremely robust.

An alternative method for removing the camera was required so that the camera could be fitted securely in a constrained space.

Made for James Dalbyhttp://dalby.tech/

  • Jesmonite Composite
  • Steel
  • Modified GoPro housing
