M2 Hospital

I assited Emma Powell designing and building an architectural model for an exhibition. Several of the windows house mobile phones that have CCTV footage from within the hospital.

The phone holders had to accomodate several sizes of phones. The roof of the model is removable to allow easy access to the electronics inside.

  • Design and CAD
  • Laser-cut Mountboard
  • Foamcore
